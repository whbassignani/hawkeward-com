My Name is Hawke. I'm a front-end designer.

About Me

Currently, I work as a Design Lead at Blizzard Entertainment, crafting tools and sites for video games in sunny, Southern California.

I manage a small group of platform designers within Battle.net across multiple product teams. Say hi!

Follow me on Twitter.

Skills & Interests

  • Platform design
  • Internal & external tools
  • Design systems
  • Marketing sites

Blizzard Battle.net Leading Platform Design Teams

Have transitioned from IC to lead?

Started with a "strike team" of designers and engineers, trying to imagine a next-generation platform for Blizzard UIs — both web and native.

Now leading multiple groups who work on CMSs for both web and esports content, as well as game developer tools and documentation.

While most of this work is behind NDA, some fruits of that tech is publicly available. See Diablo Immortal (also below), Warcraft III Reforged, and Overwatch League. More to come…
