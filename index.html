Diablo Game Sites & New Tech

The Diablo series features multiple “hack-and-slash” dungeon-crawler games. The latest entrant, Diablo Immortal is Blizzard Entertainment’s first mobile-first title.

In 2018, our team designed and shipped two announcement websites as “test pilots” for our next-gen web tech platform.

Using an internally-built design tool and a React-based system, we were able to ship these Blizzard-quality sites in half of the time… and with fewer contributors.
